🕉 The Bhagavad Gita - Voice of Eternal Wisdom

The Bhagavad Gita is a 700-verse Sanskrit scripture that is part of the Indian epic Mahabharata.
It is a sacred dialogue between Lord Krishna and Prince Arjuna, unfolding in the midst of the Kurukshetra war.

Its teachings transcend religion and offer deep insights on life, duty, and the eternal self.



Top 5 Teachings of the Gita

  1. You have the right to perform your duty, but not to the fruits of your actions.
  2. The soul is eternal and cannot be destroyed.
  3. Be free from attachment and act with discipline.
  4. See all beings with equality and compassion.
  5. Surrender to the Divine and find inner peace.
